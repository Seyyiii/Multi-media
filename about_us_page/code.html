Our Lecturer

PROFESSOR | COURSE COORDINATOR

MRS. OMIRINLEWO YINKA

Mrs. Omirinlewo is an expert in digital signal processing and interactive media. Her research focuses on the human-computer interaction paradigms in virtual reality.
She is a lecturer in the Multimedia course, where she teaches students both the theoretical and practical foundations of digital media technologies.
Her lectures emphasize creativity, technical precision, and user-centered design in multimedia systems.
Through her teaching, she equips students with the skills needed to design and analyze modern interactive media applications.
